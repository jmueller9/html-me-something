Golf Courses of St. Louis!

Pevely Farms and Aberdeen

Pevely Farms

Pevely Farms is located in the hills of Pacific Missouri and is one of my favorite courses in St. Louis. If you can get a good deal on this course it is worth every penny!

Aberdeen

Aberdeen golf course is located very close to Pevely but is different in that it is a wide open "links" style golf course! Great and challenging course! In my opinion Aberdeen is > Pevely!
